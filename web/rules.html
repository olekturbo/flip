How to Play — Flip 7

Goal

Be the first player to reach 200 points across multiple rounds.

Deck — 100 cards

  • Number cards (79): values 0–12; card N appears N times (0 appears once).
  • Action cards (9): 3× Freeze, 3× Flip 3, 3× Second Chance.
  • Positive modifier cards (6): +2, +4, +6, +8, +10, ×2.
  • Negative modifier cards (6): -2, -4, -6, -8, -10, ÷2.

The deck is not reshuffled between rounds — it carries over. It's only reshuffled when it runs out.

Each Round

  1. The dealer deals one card face-up to every player (starting left of dealer). Action cards resolve immediately during dealing.
  2. Play passes left of the dealer. On your turn you have two options:
    • Draw — take one card from the deck, then the turn passes to the next player.
    • Stay — bank your current points and exit the round (you keep your score).
  3. Number cards build your hand. Modifier and Action cards are placed above the line.
  4. After all players have either Stayed, been Frozen, or busted the round ends.

Busting

Drawing a number you already have = bust. You score 0 this round.

Only duplicate number cards cause a bust — action and modifier cards never do.

Action Cards

When you draw one, you choose any active player to receive its effect (including yourself). If you're the only active player, it targets you automatically.

Freeze Chosen player banks their points and exits the round immediately.
Flip 3 Chosen player must draw 3 cards one at a time. The sequence stops early on a bust or Flip 7. Action cards drawn during Flip 3 are deferred and resolve interactively after all non-bust draws complete.
2nd Chance Chosen player holds this card. If they would bust, discard both the duplicate and this card — they survive but their turn ends (play passes to the next player). Only one Second Chance per player at a time.

Modifier Cards

+N +2 / +4 / +6 / +8 / +10 — added to your final round score.
×2 Doubles your number-card total first, then modifiers are applied on top.
Example: numbers = 19 → ×2 = 38 → +10 = 48 pts
-N -2 / -4 / -6 / -8 / -10 — subtracted from your final round score (minimum 0).
÷2 Halves your number-card total (rounded down), then modifiers are applied on top. Cancels out with ×2 if both held.
Example: numbers = 18 → ÷2 = 9 → -4 = 5 pts

Flip 7 Bonus 🎉

Collect 7 unique number cards without busting → round ends immediately for everyone.

  • You score: number total + modifiers + +15 bonus points.
  • All other active players bank whatever they currently hold.

Scoring

  1. Sum of your number cards.
  2. Apply ×2 or ÷2 (if held; they cancel each other out).
  3. Add +modifier cards and subtract -modifier cards.
  4. Score cannot go below 0.
  5. Busted players score 0.

Winning

Game ends at the end of a round in which one player has the highest score at or above 200 points.

If two or more players are tied at 200+, the round ends normally but the game continues — keep playing until one player pulls clearly ahead.
