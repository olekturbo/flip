How to Play — Flip 7

Goal

Be the first player to reach 200 points across multiple rounds.

Deck — 106 cards

  • Number cards (79): values 0–12; card N appears N times (0 appears once).
  • Action cards (15): 3× Freeze, 3× Flip 3, 3× Second Chance, 3× Thief, 3× Swap.
  • Positive modifier cards (6): +2, +4, +6, +8, +10, ×2.
  • Negative modifier cards (6): -2, -4, -6, -8, -10, ÷2.

The deck carries over between rounds and is reshuffled only when it runs out.

Each Round

  1. The dealer deals one card face-up to every player. Action cards resolve immediately during dealing.
  2. Play passes left of dealer. On your turn:
    • Draw — take one card; turn passes to next player.
    • Stay — bank your points and exit the round.
  3. Round ends when all players have Stayed, been Frozen, or busted.

Busting

Drawing a number you already hold = bust. You score 0 this round.

Only duplicate number cards cause a bust — action and modifier cards never do.

Action Cards

Choose a target to receive the effect. Most cards target any active player including yourself. Auto-targets you if you're the only active player.

Freeze Chosen player banks their points and exits the round immediately.
Flip 3 Chosen player draws 3 cards one at a time. Stops early on bust or Flip 7. Action cards drawn during Flip 3 resolve interactively after all draws complete.
2nd Chance If holder would bust, discard both the duplicate and this card — they survive but their turn ends. During Flip 3, remaining forced draws still continue. Only one per player at a time.
Thief Choose an opponent, then pick one of their face-up number cards to steal. The stolen card moves into your hand. If this gives you 7 unique number cards, Flip 7 triggers immediately. If it would give you a duplicate, the steal is not allowed for that card.
Swap Choose any active opponent who has at least one number card (you must also hold at least one). Then pick one of your number cards and one of theirs to swap — the chosen cards trade hands. If either player receives a card they already hold, that player busts. Discarded with no effect if no valid swap exists.

Modifier Cards

+N +2 / +4 / +6 / +8 / +10 — added to your final round score.
×2 Doubles your number-card total; modifiers applied on top.
Example: 19 → ×2 = 38 → +10 = 48 pts
-N -2 / -4 / -6 / -8 / -10 — subtracted from your final round score (minimum 0).
÷2 Halves your number-card total (rounded down); modifiers applied on top. Cancels out with ×2 if both held.
Example: 18 → ÷2 = 9 → −4 = 5 pts

Flip 7 Bonus 🎉

Collect 7 unique number cards → round ends immediately for everyone.

  • You score your normal total + +15 bonus points.
  • All other active players bank whatever they currently hold.

Scoring

  1. Sum of your number cards.
  2. Apply ×2 or ÷2 if held (they cancel each other out).
  3. Add +modifiers and subtract −modifiers.
  4. Score cannot go below 0. Busted players score 0.

Winning

Game ends at the end of a round in which one player has the strictly highest score at or above 200.

If two or more players are tied at 200+, rounds continue until one player pulls clearly ahead.
