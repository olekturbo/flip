How to play

  • On your turn: Draw a card or Stop and bank your points.
  • Draw a duplicate number → bust and lose all round points.
  • Freeze: chosen player banks points and exits the round.
  • Flip 3: chosen player draws 3 extra cards immediately.
  • Second Chance: survive one bust (turn ends).
  • Modifier cards add/subtract points or double/halve your total.
  • First to 200 wins!
